## Step 1: Verify Your Site is Live

Make sure your website is accessible at `https://eqostack.com` and all pages load correctly.

---

## Step 2: Submit to Google Search Console

### A. Create Google Search Console Account

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click **"Add Property"**
4. Select **"URL prefix"**
5. Enter: `https://eqostack.com`
6. Click **"Continue"**

### B. Verify Ownership

Choose one of these methods:

**Method 1: HTML File Upload (Easiest)**
1. Download the HTML verification file from Google
2. Upload it to your website's root directory (`public/` folder)
3. Make sure it's accessible at: `https://eqostack.com/google-verification-file.html`
4. Click **"Verify"** in Google Search Console

**Method 2: HTML Tag**
1. Copy the meta tag from Google
2. Add it to your `index.html` `<head>` section
3. Click **"Verify"** in Google Search Console

**Method 3: DNS Record** (If you have domain access)
1. Add the TXT record to your domain's DNS
2. Wait for DNS propagation (can take 24-48 hours)
3. Click **"Verify"** in Google Search Console

---

## Step 3: Submit Your Sitemap

1. In Google Search Console, go to **"Sitemaps"** (left sidebar)
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click **"Submit"**
4. Google will start crawling your site

---

## Step 4: Request Indexing (Optional but Recommended)

1. In Google Search Console, go to **"URL Inspection"**
2. Enter your homepage URL: `https://eqostack.com`
3. Click **"Request Indexing"**
4. Repeat for important pages:
   - `/products`
   - `/about`
   - `/blog`

---

## Step 5: Wait and Monitor

- **Initial indexing**: Usually takes 1-7 days
- **Full indexing**: Can take 2-4 weeks
- **Monitor progress**: Check Google Search Console regularly

## Troubleshooting

**Site not showing in Google?**
- Wait at least 1-2 weeks after submission
- Check if site is indexed: Search `site:eqostack.com` in Google
- Verify sitemap is accessible
- Check for crawl errors in Search Console

**Pages not indexed?**
- Make sure pages are linked from homepage
- Submit individual URLs for indexing
- Check robots.txt isn't blocking pages
- Ensure pages have unique content
